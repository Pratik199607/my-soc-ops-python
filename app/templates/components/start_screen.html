Playful Icebreaker

Soc Ops

Find people who match the questions, mark fun squares, and race to five in a row with your friends.

How to play

  • • Search the room for the perfect social match.
  • • Tap a square when someone fits the question.
  • • Get five marked tiles in a line and celebrate bingo.

Why it’s fun

This is a playful way to learn about new people, spark conversations, and watch the board fill up.
